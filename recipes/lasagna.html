Classic Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish made with layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella. It's baked to perfection and perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or just a comforting meal after a long day. This classic recipe combines the rich flavors of beef, tomatoes, and Italian herbs for a satisfying and delicious experience.

Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite ingredients. Some variations include adding vegetables like spinach, mushrooms, or zucchini for a more nutritious twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese together.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread a third of the ricotta mixture, followed by a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers two more times, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
