Loaded Nachos

Description

Nachos are a popular snack or appetizer made from crispy tortilla chips topped with melted cheese, and often loaded with a variety of savory toppings. This easy-to-make recipe takes nachos to the next level with flavorful ground beef, fresh salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.

Whether you're hosting a party, watching a game, or just craving a cheesy treat, these nachos are sure to satisfy. Customize them with your favorite toppings, such as jalapeños, olives, or beans!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup guacamole
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced jalapeños (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat and add the taco seasoning, following package instructions to prepare the beef mixture.
  3. Spread a layer of tortilla chips on a baking sheet or oven-safe dish.
  4. Top the chips with the seasoned ground beef, spreading it evenly across the chips.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar and Monterey Jack cheese evenly over the beef and chips.
  6. Place the nachos in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with salsa, sour cream, guacamole, chopped red onion, jalapeños (if using), and cilantro.
  8. Serve immediately with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor!
